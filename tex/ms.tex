Let the squashing function be
\begin{align}
S(x) &=
    \left\{
        \begin{array}{lr}
            \log (1 + x), & \textnormal{if } x \geq 0, \\
            -\log(1 - x), & \textnormal{otherwise}.
        \end{array}
    \right.
\end{align}
Operations on the squashed grade can be defined in terms of logsumexp
and logdiffexp, to avoid overflow.
